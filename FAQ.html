Information About the Art

Drawings and Collages are created on acid free artist quality paper.

Colour Representation:

Although I endeavour to represent the truest colours of the actual artwork on screen, the nature of digital computer screens means colours may vary slightly.

Framing:

All work is shipped unframed and in most cases unmatted. While framing is always a personal choice I suggest following the art museum model of providing a generously spaced matt (approx. 5” or 12cm) and a plain wooden (black or natural) frame.

Payment & Shipping

Privacy/Security:

Paypal is the most secure way of shopping online. When you purchase using Paypal, a buyer's information is 100% secure and is not shared with the seller. None of your information can be used outside this site and credit card details are not collected.

Payment:

All payments are taken in Australian Dollars ($AUD), and are charged at the current exchange rate.

Shipping:

Shipping is free anywhere in Australia. International Shipping is approximately $40.00 AUD and will take roughly 8-21 business days. (Please arrange for international shipping by email.) All orders receive a tracking number with Australia Post. The buyer must provide an accurate full address by email. Each sold work will be packaged in a plastic sleeve between stiff cardboard in a soft protective envelope. Works will be shipped free via regular Post Office unless the buyer requests by email a private courier. If this is the case, the buyer will absorb this cost. All sales are final.
