INFORMATION ABOUT THE ART

Drawings and Collages are created on acid free artist quality paper.

Colour Representation:

Although I endeavour to represent the truest colours of the actual artwork on screen, the nature of digital computer screens means colours may vary slightly.

Framing:

All work is shipped unframed and in most cases unmatted. While framing is always a personal choice I suggest following the art museum model of providing a generously spaced matt (approx. 5” or 12cm) and a plain wooden (black or natural) frame.
